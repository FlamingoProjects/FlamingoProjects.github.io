What Can We Do?

There, at this point, is a great amount that we can do. We can really change this impact that we have made on this world and the animals and wildlife of this world with the distribution and use of plastic. Now although it might be impossible to go back to the wonders of the environment before plastic we can really undo most of its large effects and keep things from getting worse.
